About NMTA

Our Constitution & Core Objectives

Established by the traders of New Market, Pakyong, the New Market Traders Association (NMTA) is an independent, non-governmental organization dedicated to the unity, welfare, and prosperity of the regional business community. We stand committed to serving our members without political affiliation, driven solely by the goal of collective advancement.

Our Mission

To safeguard the rights and interests of traders in New Market, Pakyong, creating a fair, competitive, and ethical business environment for all.

Key Objectives

  • Unity: Fostering collaboration to resolve common challenges.
  • Growth: Organizing workshops and training for skill enhancement.
  • Advocacy: Liaising with government bodies on trade matters.

President's Message

"At NMTA, we believe that a strong community is built on trust, transparency, and mutual support. Together, we are not just traders; we are the architects of our region's economic future. Let us move forward with unity and purpose."

Geeta Poudyal

President, NMTA

Our Vision & Future Goals

Building a stronger, healthier, and more prosperous business community in Pakyong.

Business Workshops

Workshops and training sessions to strengthen business knowledge, skillsets, and market competitiveness.

Corporate Insurance

Providing insurance options and financial protection for members’ businesses and assets.

Shop Security

Mandating CCTV installation for all member shops to enhance security and prevent losses.

Financial Support

Providing low-interest loans (10%) to members and collective fundraising for emergencies or expansion.
